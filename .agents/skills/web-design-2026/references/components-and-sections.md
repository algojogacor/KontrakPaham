## UX Pattern Modern

- **Personalized content / AI-driven personalization** — konten menyesuaikan user/behavior/lokasi, tapi harus terasa membantu bukan menyeramkan (privacy-conscious personalization).
- **Adaptive UI, predictive UX, context-aware interface**.
- **Progressive disclosure** — info kompleks ditampilkan bertahap, reduced cognitive load.
- **Clear visual hierarchy, strong CTA hierarchy, frictionless conversion, one-click action**.
- **Smart onboarding, guided flow**.
- **Empty state informatif, error state membantu, success state jelas**.
- **Trust signal placement, social proof, comparison before purchase**.
- **Mobile-first UX**: thumb-friendly interaction, gesture-based interaction, bottom sheet, floating action button.
- **Voice interaction, accessibility-first UX**.

## AI dalam Website Modern

AI chatbot/assistant/copilot, AI search/semantic search, AI recommendation/product finder, AI content personalization, AI form assistant, AI FAQ assistant, AI summarizer, AI onboarding/lead qualification, AI writing helper, AI-based user segmentation, AI-powered A/B testing, AI accessibility helper.

**Penting**: selalu ada human-in-the-loop / fallback ke manusia untuk AI yang menentukan keputusan penting (mis. hasil analisis kontrak, rekomendasi harga). Jangan biarkan AI jalan tanpa transparansi ke user.
